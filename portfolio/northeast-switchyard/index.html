Northeast 230/115kv Switchyard

Northeast Switchyard was a 230/115kv switchyard upgrade where crews worked daily in close proximity to both 230kv and 115kv energized bus and circuits. Being a high profile / high visibility project to our client, our employees worked under a very strict and strategically sequenced environment. Our crew met every milestone to our customer’s complete satisfaction, all while recording “Zero” incidents, damage, safety violation or injuries.

  • 230kv Terminal Addition
  • 115kv Terminal Removal
  • 230 Equipment Removal & Installation (Breakers, Switches, Metering, etc.)
  • 115kv Equipment and Bus removal
  • 230kv Bus Installation
  • Lightning Shielding Installation
  • Conduit and Cable Installation (including termination)
  • Ground Grid Installation
