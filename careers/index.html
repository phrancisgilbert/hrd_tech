Careers

Careers – HRD Technology is a Community Company

Community means we care about the people we work for and the people we work with. At HRD Technology, we want you to enjoy coming to work each day. We believe the people you work with for forty or more hours a week should be like a second family.

While we are a tight-knit group at HRD Technology, it’s important to go home feeling good about what you do; that’s why we provide health care, retirement and we understand time off. Life happens, and we’re a company that is all about providing understanding for our employees.

If you are dedicated and committed to doing your job the best it can be done, you are right for the HRD Technology family. Fill out the application below and attach it, along with your resume where it says “Apply Now.” If you are applying for a construction job, please include the Construction Experience Form.

HRD Technology is a proud member of the Western Electrical Contractors Association. We participate in the state approved Electrical Apprenticeship program.

If you are considering being an electrician for your career choice, and would like to receive information on the apprenticeship program, click here WECA.
