What We Do

Electrical Contractors – We Specialize in Constructing the Infrastructure that Delivers Energy to America®

HRD Technology is a specialty electrical contractor, with capabilities in general engineering and contracting. We primarily serve clients in the Utility, Wind and Solar energy sectors and General Industrial and Commercial for federal and other select clients. Whether it’s constructing a utility scale solar plant, electrically connecting new wind turbine plant, or constructing and rehabilitating utility substations and transmission lines, HRD Technology uses expertise to execute the work. Our client list spans from many of America’s investor-owned, municipal and rural co-op utilities, to renewable energy clients such as wind energy general contractors, solar project developers, and the federal government.

Our team has constructed more than 40 electrical substations and hundreds of miles of transmission line over the past ten years, with projects consistently completed on time or ahead of schedule. We have an outstanding safety record supported by an IIPP plan which leads the industry. Our experience, our people, and our professionalism make HRD Technology the preferred choice with customers. Part of HRD Technology’s strength is that we are independently owned, financially secure and fully insured and bondable for significant project sizes.
