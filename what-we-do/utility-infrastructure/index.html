Utility Infrastructure

HRD Technology’s roots and core competency lie in the construction of utility infrastructure

Over the past decade, we have constructed new substations, transmission lines and upgraded stations and lines as utility companies across America update and upgrade their systems. Our ability to work across the country competitively has allowed HRD Technology to serve a far ranging variety of utility customers; consistently meeting their needs and exceeding their expectations.
