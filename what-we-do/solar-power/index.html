Solar Power

Cutting-edge solar power allows HRD Technology to do what we do best; Deliver the Future of Energy.

HRD Technology is dedicated to making energy count, and that means answering the demand for solar power. We are at the forefront of both commercial and utility scale installations, and known as a leader in providing our customers with the option of replacing fossil fuel energy with electricity generated from photovoltaic cells. Whether your system is roof mounted, a fixed tilt or tracking system, our team has the experience to meet your project needs.
